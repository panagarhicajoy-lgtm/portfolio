My Journey ✨

From healthcare to tech, my path has been anything but straight - and I wouldn't have it any other way!

2019-2020

Senior High School Graduate

Completed my SHS education at Cagayan Valley Institute (CVI) where I developed strong communication skills and learned the value of active listening - qualities that serve me well in both tech and life.

2020-2022

Nursing Student (Midwifery)

I studied midwifery because I loved helping people. Those two years taught me to stay calm under pressure, notice small details, and care deeply—skills I didn’t realize would also make me a better programmer. When health challenges changed my path, I found the same problem-solving focus in coding. Now, instead of supporting mothers, I build solutions with code—still using the same care and precision, just in a different way.!

2022

Midwifery to Tech

When health issues forced me to step away from midwifery, I was heartbroken. But I soon discovered that the skills I loved in healthcare—solving problems, staying calm under pressure, and paying attention to every detail—were just as valuable in tech. Now, instead of tracking labor progress, I debug code. Instead of handling emergencies in the delivery room, I troubleshoot errors in my programs. The tools changed, but my love for solving problems didn’t. Sometimes life redirects us—and that’s okay. I’m still helping people, just in a different way.!

2023-2024

First Year BSIT & Secretary

As a fresh BSIT student, I embraced the challenge of learning to code while serving as class secretary. Juggling minutes and methods, I learned organization and teamwork in a tech context.

2025-Present

Third Year Representative

Now representing our department, I bridge communication between students and faculty. My diverse background helps me understand different perspectives and find creative solutions.

Why Tech?

My winding path has given me a unique perspective. In healthcare, I learned about systems and precision. In tech, I found a field where I can apply that same care to create solutions that help people in different ways.
